# Lines starting with # are ignored as comments
# Each line is comma separated
# This simple approach means it can be Question, ChoiceA, ChoiceB, etc.
# We use the notion that the first choice is the answer.
What is 1+1?, 2, 1, 3, 4
What is your favorite color?, Yellow, Blue, Green, Red, Orange, Magenta
What is the capital of France?, Paris, Europe, London, Marseille

# If your question or answer has a comma in it, use quotes
"So, which algorithm is faster for sorting in general?", Merge Sort, Bubble Sort, Selection Sort, Insertion Sort, Shell Sort
